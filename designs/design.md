# Project Brief & PRD: ARMS (Attendance & Resource Management System)

## 1. Project Overview

ARMS is a high-utility mobile application designed for educational institutions to manage attendance, student leaves, exam records, and marks entry. The primary goal is to provide a "Clean Utility" experience that prioritizes speed, scannability, and ease of use in fast-paced environments.

## 2. Design Language: "Clean Utility"

- **Visual Direction:** High-density, professional, and structured, resembling a well-printed form.
- **Color Palette:**
    - **Primary Brand Color:** `#0051d5` (ARMS Blue) used for primary actions and branding.
    - **Surface:** Pure White (`#ffffff`) for backgrounds to maintain a clean aesthetic.
    - **Secondary Surfaces:** `#f5f5f5` for card backgrounds to provide subtle contrast.
    - **Semantic Colors:** Red for Absent/Errors, Green for Present/Success.
- **Typography:** Plus Jakarta Sans / Hanken Grotesk for a modern, readable feel.
- **Corner Radius:** 8px (`ROUND_EIGHT`) across buttons, inputs, and cards for consistency.
- **Navigation:** Truecaller-style bottom navigation with the profile icon positioned on the right.
